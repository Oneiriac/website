Discord

Discord is a free video, voice and text chat service.

WIRED has it's own Discord server which has channels for:

  • Job listings
  • Interactions with our sponsors
  • Programming help
  • Event planning
  • FIT subject discussion
  • off-topic free talk

Rules

Note that these rules are subject to change as deemed appropriate by WIRED.

  • Be a WIRED member. You must be a WIRED member to be in the Discord channel. Verification using your student email may be required.
  • No collusion. While academic discussion is encouraged, cheating on assignments is not allowed.
  • Be nice. Bad faith participation, insults, and bigotry will result in a removal from the Discord

Verification

We require people to verify their Discord against our membership to particpate in Discord fully. Type !verify in the #welcome channel to verify your account. We won't share your Discord handle unless required to by law enforcement, MSA, or Monash University.
