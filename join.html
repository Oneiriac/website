Why join?

WIRED Monash is the principal student society for students in the Faculty of Information Technology and those who share a curiosity for all things digital.

Events and Competitions

We have many events and competitions this year to cater for everyone - from UNIHACK to CySCA, Trivia Night to Industry Night.

Free BBQs

We hold a couple of BBQs during each semester - and all of them are free for members. Who doesn't love free food?

Internships and Careers

Looking for a job or want to build up your resume? We will notify you of any internships or career opportunities that comes.

Academic Support

If you have any questions about your course or subject, we will try to help and point you in the right direction.

Sign up!

Membership costs $7 or just $2 with a MSA membership.
